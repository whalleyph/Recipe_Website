So you like SPaghetti, huh?

Spaghetti is so nice and yummy.

Here's how to make it!

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 50g pancetta
  • 75g parmesan cheese

Process:

  1. Cook the pancetta until nice and crispy
  2. Cook the SPaghets
  3. Stir in egg yolk, cheese and pancetts
  4. Serve and enjoy

Not for vegetarians.
